Delete Account

Request deletion of your OVOMonie account

If you no longer wish to use OVOMonie, you can request deletion of your account by contacting our support team. For a financial service, certain records may be retained where required by law or regulation.

How to request deletion

  • Email admin@ovomonie.com from the email linked to your account.
  • Include your full name and registered phone number.
  • Support may ask you to verify your identity before the request is processed.

What is deleted

  • Account profile information no longer required for retention.
  • App preferences associated with the account.
  • Non-retained support data.

What may be retained

  • Transaction, audit, and settlement records required by law.
  • KYC and identity verification records where retention is required.
  • Fraud prevention and compliance records for the applicable retention period.

How long it takes

Deletion is usually completed within 14 business days after verification.
